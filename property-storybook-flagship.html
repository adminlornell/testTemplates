The Story

Where History Meets Opportunity

Built in 1900, 415 Main Street has stood as a cornerstone of Worcester's downtown for over a century.

A Legacy Building with Modern Potential
This historic 4-story masonry building has been a fixture in Worcester's medical community for decades. With 87% occupancy and a strong tenant base, it represents a rare opportunity to acquire a cash-flowing asset at an exceptional price point.

The property sits in the heart of Worcester Metro, Massachusetts' second-largest city and a growing hub for healthcare, education, and biotechnology. With Worcester Polytechnic Institute nearby and a thriving medical corridor, this location offers unparalleled stability and growth potential.

"At just $8.11 per square foot, this represents one of the most compelling value-add opportunities in the Worcester market. The fundamentals are strong, the location is exceptional, and the upside is significant."

The Investment Thesis
With 3,495 SF of vacant space and below-market rents in place, there's immediate opportunity to increase NOI through strategic lease-up and modest rent adjustments. Questions & Answers

Everything You Need to Know

What is the asking price for 415 Main Street?
The asking price is $219,000, which equals $8.11 per square foot - an exceptional value for a 27,012 SF medical office building in Worcester's downtown area. Comparable buildings in the market are trading at $15-20/SF, making this a compelling value-add opportunity.
What is the current occupancy rate?
The building is currently 87% occupied with stable medical tenants, providing immediate cash flow. There is 3,495 SF of vacant space (12.94% vacancy) which presents an immediate opportunity to increase NOI through strategic lease-up.
What is the potential ROI for this investment?
With current market rents ranging from $17-21/SF and the property trading at $8.11/SF, there is significant upside potential. By leasing up the vacant space and bringing existing leases to market rates, investors can expect to increase NOI by 20-30%. Detailed financial projections are available in the Investment Memorandum for registered users.
What type of tenants occupy the building?
The building primarily houses medical offices and healthcare-related tenants. The property is equipped with bio-tech/lab space capabilities, 24-hour access, and medical-grade infrastructure, making it attractive to healthcare providers, medical practices, and life sciences companies.
What are the building's key features?
Built in 1900 with solid masonry construction, the building features 4 floors, 4 elevators, 24-hour access, bio-tech/lab space capabilities, security system, reception area, and storage space. The typical floor size is 4,800 SF, allowing for flexible tenant configurations.
Is financing available for this property?
Yes, commercial financing options are available for qualified buyers. Given the strong occupancy rate and cash flow, this property should qualify for favorable commercial loan terms. We can connect you with preferred lenders who specialize in medical office buildings and have competitive rates.
Can I schedule a private tour?
Absolutely! We encourage all serious investors to tour the property in person. Contact us to schedule a private showing at your convenience. We can accommodate evening and weekend appointments and provide access to all floors and common areas.

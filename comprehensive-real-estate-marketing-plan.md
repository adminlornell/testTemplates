Based on analysis of your 20-property portfolio spanning Worcester County, Providence, and Cambridge markets, this marketing plan leverages your diverse property mix (Office, Industrial, Retail, Flex, and Multifamily) with total value exceeding $8.5M in active listings. ### Target Audience Personas

#### **Persona A: "The Expansion Executive"** (Industrial/Flex Focus)
- **Demographics:** 45-60 years, Business owners/C-suite
- **Company Size:** $5M-50M revenue
- **Pain Points:** Outgrowing current space, need loading/shipping capabilities
- **Desires:** Scalable space, highway access, cost efficiency
- **Decision Factors:** Cap rate, tenant improvements, lease flexibility

#### **Persona B: "The Local Entrepreneur"** (Retail/Small Office Focus)
- **Demographics:** 30-50 years, Small business owners
- **Budget:** $150K-500K purchase or $15-25/SF lease
- **Pain Points:** High rent in metro areas, limited visibility
- **Desires:** Main Street presence, affordable entry, community connection
- **Decision Factors:** Foot traffic, parking, total occupancy cost

#### **Persona C: "The Portfolio Builder"** (Investment/Multifamily Focus)
- **Demographics:** 35-65 years, Real estate investors
- **Portfolio Size:** 2-20 properties
- **Pain Points:** Finding value in competitive market, management complexity
- **Desires:** Cash flow, appreciation potential, diversification
- **Decision Factors:** NOI, cap rate, value-add opportunities
